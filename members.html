Our core members are the backbone of Zamac Nepal, driven by a passion for scale model collecting and a selfless commitment to the community. With a non-profit ethos, they dedicate their time and effort to fostering collaboration, organizing events, and supporting collectors of all levels. Their unwavering dedication ensures that Zamac Nepal remains a vibrant and inclusive space for all enthusiasts.
