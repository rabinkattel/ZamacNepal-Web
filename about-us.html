OUR STORY

The Nepalese Scale Models Collectors Community (Zamac Nepal) was founded with the vision of bringing together scale model enthusiasts from across Nepal. What started as a small group of passionate collectors has grown into a vibrant community that celebrates the artistry and craftsmanship of scale models.

Our mission is to provide a platform for collectors to showcase their collections, connect with like-minded individuals, and inspire the next generation of hobbyists. From hosting exhibitions to organizing meet-ups, we aim to create memorable experiences for our members.

Join us in our journey as we continue to nurture and grow Nepal's scale model collector community.
